SkongaWeb.com

Welcome to SkongaWeb, the platform for managing schools operation designed to support schools at all levels in Tanzania. The system features includes:

  • Student Registration
  • Student Academic Performance
  • Student Fees Payment Management
  • Parent's Feedback
  • Notice Board

The system has been designed to accomodate requirements of Tanzania Primary and Secondary schools education system.
